About me

When I am not working, I am helping my wife raise our children, volunteering at my church (the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints) as Elder's Quorum President, cooking, reading books, listening to books and podcasts, tweeting, or (very occasionally) blogging. I am an advocate of intermittent fasting and low-carbohydrate dieting (although I don't always do a great job of adhering).
